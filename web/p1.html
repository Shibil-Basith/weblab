About Us

City Football Group (CFG) is a holding company established to oversee the creation and administration of a network of linked clubs and other football-related businesses.

CFG is majority-owned by Abu Dhabi United Group (ADUG), an investment and development company privately owned by Sheikh Mansour bin Zayed Al Nahyan, a member of the Abu Dhabi royal family.

CFG's aim is to develop a sustainable and successful model for football, based around three main pillars: performance, people, and purpose.
